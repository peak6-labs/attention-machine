## Tier 1: Highest ROI (This Week)

### Referral Program
- Friend's list of ~1,000 Austin founders in a pitching community
- Offer $100–500 per accepted applicant (Gauntlet pays $1,000/referral as reference point)
- Email blast alone could get 5–10% of total goal

### LinkedIn Job Posting
- Post on PEAK6 company page as "Entrepreneur in Residence" or similar
- Get Peak6Trials listed on PEAK6 careers page
- Free/cheap, high intent traffic

### Newsletter Ads
- TLDR Founders, The Hustle, Morning Brew (startup editions)
- Targeted and measurable
- Run staggered over 4–6 weeks

### X/Twitter Push
- Get Austen + PEAK6 execs to RT
- Pin the post

## Tier 2: Medium Effort, High Yield
