# Peak6 Trials — Ideal Customer Profile

## Learnings from Launch (as of 3/17/26)

- Messaging is unclear — need crisper personas and understanding of where founders show up
- M&J + PEAK6 socials not enough — need to hit key points on why M&J and why PEAK6 is 1 of 1
- Fintech + Austin are barriers to application — need to tackle head on in messaging
- Team distribution not enough — need to hone in on PR + new moments of engagement

## Renewed Strategy

- **Personas** — Focus: start with much more targeted analysis of where candidates are
- **Marketing** — Experimentation: use as a way to test messaging, figure out A/B testing
- **PR** — Amplification: shift focus here after experimenting with different messages

---

## Clear Value Props (all personas)

- Shadow our employees, learn from us then sell to us
- We are not a VC. No LPs, all our capital
- Cover your bills. Living wage in Austin
- It's okay to not have an idea or not know Fintech, let us get you there
- We hired AI engineers ready to be your cofounders
