# Peak6 Trials — Ideal Customer Profile

## Demographics

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 22–35 |
| Experience | Some work experience, not necessarily in fintech or tech |
| Location | Austin-based or willing to relocate |
| Technical | Non-technical founders welcome — no coding required |

## Mindset

- Wants to start a company but lacks resources, capital, or network
- Ambitious and action-oriented
- Curious about fintech (defined broadly as "anything involving money movement and management")
- Willing to commit full-time for 12 months

## Situation

- Grinding at a job they're overqualified for
- Between things — just left a role or looking for the next move
- May have tried building something before but didn't have the runway
- Could be a failed founder looking for round two

## The Pitch

> "You want to be a founder but don't have the runway. We give you $100k, resources, and a real shot."

## Key Points

- No prior fintech experience required
- No technical skills required
- Can arrive with an existing idea or find problems to solve within PEAK6's network
- This is not an accelerator — it's a 12-month residency with real salary and equity

## Open Questions (to sharpen ICP further)

- Technical vs. non-technical split — are we targeting builders or hustlers/ops/product people?
- Age/career stage sweet spot — fresh grads? Mid-career pivots?
- Idea stage — people with ideas, or blank slates pointed at PEAK6 problems?
- What traits do the best applicants from the first 40 share?
