**Duration:** 12 Months, Full-Time

## About PEAK6 Trials

PEAK6 Trials is a first-of-its-kind founder residency backed by PEAK6 Investments — a 29-year-old firm with 15+ businesses and multiple multi-billion dollar companies in its portfolio. PEAK6 has never had a down year.

Co-founded by Matt Hulsizer and Jenny Just, Trials is built on a simple belief: *Trials don't break founders. They make them.*

This is **not** an accelerator. There are no cohorts, no demo days, no pitch competitions. This is a 12-month residency where you build a real company with real resources behind you.

## What You Get

- **$100,000 annual salary** for the full 12-month residency
- **Up to 51% equity** at a $1 billion valuation — the structure is inverted from traditional VC. You build equity as you remove risk and drive revenue
- **In-house AI engineers** to develop initial prototypes (with potential to transition into a CTO role)
- **Engineering, legal, and compliance resources** tailored for fintech
- **Direct access to PEAK6's network** — established distribution channels and potential customers
- **PEAK6 deploys its own earned capital** — no fund cycles, no LP constraints

## Who This Is For

- Future (and past) founders ready to build
- You want to start a company but don't have the runway, resources, or network to do it alone
- You may or may not have an idea — you can bring one or identify problems to solve within PEAK6's ecosystem
- **No fintech experience required**
- **No technical background required** — we're looking for business-minded founders, not just engineers

## What We're Looking For

- Genuine enthusiasm for building in fintech (broadly: anything involving money movement and management)
- Grit, resourcefulness, and a bias toward action
- Willingness to commit full-time, in-person in Austin for 12 months
- The kind of person who's overqualified for their current role — or between things and ready to bet on themselves
